our story

Started on a small balcony in Mylapore.

Little Knot began with one wooden table, a roll of twine and too many candles. Today it's still small — just two of us picking, packing and writing every note by hand.

Every order is wrapped the same evening. If you message us on WhatsApp, you'll get one of us — not a bot.
